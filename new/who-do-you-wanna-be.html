who do you
wanna be

How can a character,
especially a female one,
persist as pixel?

Extending the concerns of the previous series, who do you wanna be considers how characters might come into being, circulate, and endure through the logic of the pixel.

Focusing in particular on female figures, the project asks how identity is stylized, flattened, and repeatedly reconstructed as an image within digital space.

Between performance and projection, these characters appear not as fixed selves but as mutable surfaces, negotiating visibility, desire, and presence through their pixelated form.
